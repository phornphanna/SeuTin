Why We're Here

Over the last twenty years healthcare in Cambodia has improved significantly. However, children still die from preventable causes.
Access to quality healthcare within Cambodia is not yet universal. There is a lack of care available for children with long-term, rare or acute conditions – particularly for those in the poorest and most remote villages.
